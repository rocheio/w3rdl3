How to Play

  • Swipe or use arrow keys to move tiles
    • New A and B tiles will spawn randomly
  • Tiles combine when they have no space to move
    • A tiles will not combine with other A tiles
    • B tiles will not combine with other B tiles
  • Type a 5-letter word from letters on the board to score
  • Get as many words as you can in 125 moves

About w3rdl3

This is just Wordle and Threes combined.

If you enjoy w3rdl3, please check out the original inspirations!
